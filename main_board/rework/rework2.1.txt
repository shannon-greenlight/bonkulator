Rework 4 boards. Boards are approx 72x110mm, 4-layer.

1. Replace 0805 SMD 10K with 56K. 
	a. R93, R94, R95, R96, R121, R122, R123, R124, R139, R140
2. Add through-hole components.
	a. 14 3.5mm Jacks
	b. 1 4-pin header
	c. 24 LEDs -- see note
	d. 1 Rotary Encoder
	e. 2 Test Points
	f. 16 Pushbuttons

note:
	4 LEDs require hand-wiring to swap position with adjacent LED.
	D37 <-> D19
	D38 <-> D20
